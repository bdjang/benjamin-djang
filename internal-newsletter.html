HTML Email: Internal Newsletter

Launched: Ongoing since September 2012

At Democracy Prep, I manage a newsletter sent to our internal staff. Through this project, I learned about designing and coding HTML emails and using email data to improve open rates. Below is an overview of how the newsletter has changed and developed.

Template #1

BACKGROUND:

Through a WSYIWYG editor and an email template from Verticalrepsonse.com, I edited text and photos and launched this email to our internal staff.

Redesign #1

BACKGROUND:

I designed and coded a newsletter template based on the specifications of our growing organization. Modern HTML Email introduced me to coding table-based email templates and email client and mobile device code requirements.

Redesign #2

BACKGROUND:

After attending the Responsive Email Newsletter Design workshop, I redesigned the newsletter to be more user friendly and mobile responsive for our users.
